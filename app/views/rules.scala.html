Rules

The first player who reaches the destination with all his tokens is the winner.



Move according to this list:

  • 2 - 10: Move according to card value.
  • Jack: Switch your token with one from another player.
  • Queen: Move 12 squares.
  • King: Move 13 squares or bring one token into the game.
  • Ace: Move 1 square or bring one token into the game.
  • Joker: Use as any other card.

If you're not able to move, you have to discard them. You then have to wait until all other players have used their cards.
